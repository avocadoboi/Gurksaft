Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From *sigällä, the adessive case of *sikä- (the root of se extended with -ka; found in sikäläinen). Compare Erzya сия (śija, “there”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsie̯lːæ/, [ˈs̠ie̞̯lːæ]
  • Rhymes: -ielːæ
  • Syllabification(key): siel‧lä

Adverb[edit]

siellä

  1. (static) there (when the speaker does not point at the place)
    Me olimme siellä. (cf. sinne)
    We were there.
